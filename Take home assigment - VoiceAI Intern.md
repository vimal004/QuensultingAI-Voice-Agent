# **Functional Requirements**

**Your voice agent should be able to:**

* Handle inbound calls naturally  
* Book appointments  
* Answer frequently asked questions  
* Handle interruptions gracefully  
* Handle fallback scenarios  
* Collect caller details  
* Escalate or transfer the call to a human when necessary

---

# **Technical Requirements**

## **RetellAI**

**Your solution must:**

* Be built using RetellAI Conversational Flow  
* Use proper conversation nodes and branching  
* Demonstrate good conversation design

**Prompt-only implementations will not be considered.**

## **Automation**

**Use either:**

* n8n  
* Python (FastAPI, Flask, Django, or any framework of your choice)

## **Mandatory Integrations**

**Your solution must:**

* Save caller information into Google Sheets or Airtable  
* Trigger a confirmation email or webhook after a successful appointment booking
